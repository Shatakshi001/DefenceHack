HTML Media Tag

The media attribute specifies what media/device the linked document is optimized for. This attribute is used to specify that the target URL is designed for special devices (like iPhone) , speech or print media.

Video Tag

The star of the show is the <video> tag. At its simplest, it just needs to know the source of the video file. We need to add player controls. The browser has a beautiful set of default controls built-in, and we can turn them on with a simple attribute.

Audio Tag

We embed a sound file (like a song or a podcast) with player controls.

The Professional way to embed media

Many times the browser do not support a specific type of the video, then in that case we give few more options to play the video in different formats. Like /mp4, /mp3, /webm, /ogg.
